THE GIRL ON THE TRAIN

The girl on the train by paula hawkins is a psychological thriller. Centered on rachel watson a troubled woman struggling with alcoholism and the aftermath of her failed marriage everyday she texts the train into london passing by the same row of houses. She becomes obsessed with one seemingly perfect couple, whom she names jess and jason. In reality, they are megan and scott hipwell. One day rachel sees something shocking from the train window involving megan and a mysterious man. shortly after megan goes missing. rachel, desperate to uncover the truth and prove her worth, becomes entangled in investigation. however her real unreliable memory, due to blackouts from drinking, comcomplicates her efforts.
as the story unfolds, the narrative shifts between rachel, megan (in fflashbacks),and anna, the new wife of rachel's ex-husband,tom. this multi-perspective storytelling gradually reveals dark secrets about the charecters and their interwined lives.
the novel explores theme of obsession, deception, and the fallibillity of memory, leading to a gripping and suspenseful conclusion as racals unravels the mystery of megan's dissappearence and uncovers shocking truths about those around her.
